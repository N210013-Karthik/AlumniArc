About Rajiv Gandhi University of Knowledge and Technologies(RGUKT)

Mission Vision History Campus

Our Mission

"To provide world-class education and training in the fields of engineering, technology, and sciences to the students of rural India, especially from underserved areas, through innovative teaching methodologies. The university is committed to fostering academic excellence, research, and holistic development that will empower its students to make meaningful contributions to the nation and the global community."

Our Vision

"To be a premier institution of higher education dedicated to the pursuit of excellence in teaching, research, and innovation. RGUKT envisions empowering students from rural and underprivileged backgrounds with the knowledge and skills required to excel in global technological advancements, fostering creativity, leadership, and social responsibility to meet the evolving needs of society."

Our History

Rajiv Gandhi University of Knowledge and Technologies (RGUKT), Nuzvidu, was established in 2008 by the Government of Andhra Pradesh with a vision to provide quality education to the talented youth from rural and underserved areas. The university was created with the aim of bridging the educational divide by offering cutting-edge programs in the fields of engineering, technology, and sciences.

Our Campus

Located in Nuzvidu, Andhra Pradesh, the RGUKT campus provides state-of-the-art facilities for students. With modern classrooms, laboratories, and recreational spaces, the campus offers a conducive environment for learning and personal development.
